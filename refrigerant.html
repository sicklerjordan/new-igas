R-22

R-22 is an HCFC refrigerant used for residential and commercial air conditioning and for medium- and low-temperature commercial refrigeration applications.

R-134A

R-134a is one of the worlds most used refrigerants, as it is very diverse and widely embedded in automotive, commercial, and residential air conditioning systems, across the world.

R-404A

R-404A is a non-ozone-depleting replacement for R-502 and R-22 used in commercial refrigerator equipment for low and medium temperature ranges.

R-407A

R-407A is a non-ozone-depleting replacement for R-404A and R-507 in system designs or from existing R-22 refrigeration units.

R-407C

R-407C is a non-ozone-depleting R-22 replacement because of its similar cooling capacity, energy efficiency, and pressures as R-22 in systems.

R-410A

R-410A is the leading HFC refrigerant replacing R-22 in residential and commercial AC, as well as heat pump, applications.

R-422B

R-422B is a non-ozone-depleting refrigerant compatible with all standard oils, equipment components, and materials used in R-22 systems.

R-422D

R-422D is a non-ozone-depleting, multi-purpose refrigerant that works in existing low and medium temperature direct expansion refrigeration applications, including commercial supermarket systems.

R-438A

R-438A is a non-ozone-depleting R-22 replacement and has the closest performance match to R-22 of all no oil change replacement refrigerants.

R-507A

R-507 is a non-ozone-depleting refrigerant primarily designed to replace R-502 in low- and medium-temperature commercial refrigeration applications.
