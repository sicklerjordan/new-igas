iClaimTM   – Reclaim Our Future

Recovering, reclaiming, and repurposing HFC, HCFC, and CFC gases to minimize waste, minimize virgin production, as well as separate mixed gases and remove contaminants to maximize the lifecycle of refrigerants.

iClaim

Refrigerant Compensation

iGas can purchase refrigerants of any condition as part of maintaining the lifecycle of refrigerants. We accept and compensate for any purity mixture of any refrigerants at no charge. Plus, we provide prepaid freight!

iClaim

Refrigerant Separation

iClaim™ has on-site capabilities to separate reclaimable components, remove contaminants, and can recover any purity of refrigerant to maintain the lifecycle of refrigerant and sustainability.

iClaim

Reclaimed Refrigerant

As supplies of virgin HFC refrigerant are reduced, the availability of reclaimed refrigerant to service existing equipment in future years is vital. iGas is recovering, reclaiming, and repurposing HFC, HCFC, and CFC gases to minimize waste, minimize virgin production, as well as separate mixed gases and remove contaminants to maximize the lifecycle of refrigerants.
